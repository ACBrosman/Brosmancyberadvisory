Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our advisory services and engagement process

How quickly can you start an engagement?

For most advisory services, we can begin within 1-2 weeks of contract signing. Emergency incident response services are available 24/7 with immediate response capabilities.

Do you work with small businesses or only enterprises?

We work with organizations of all sizes, from small businesses to large enterprises. Our advisory services are tailored to match your organization's size, complexity, and budget.

What information do you need to provide a quote?

We typically need to understand your organization size, current security posture, specific challenges, desired outcomes, and timeline. A brief consultation call helps us provide an accurate proposal.

Do you sign NDAs and confidentiality agreements?

Absolutely. We understand the sensitive nature of cybersecurity work and are happy to sign NDAs and other confidentiality agreements before any detailed discussions.

Can you provide references from similar organizations?

Yes, we can provide references from clients in similar industries or with comparable security challenges, subject to their approval and our confidentiality agreements.
